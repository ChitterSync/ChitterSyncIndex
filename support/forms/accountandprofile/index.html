Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to your account settings and click "Reset Password."

How do I update my email address?

Navigate to your profile settings and update your email under "Contact Information."

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team at support@chittersync.com.
