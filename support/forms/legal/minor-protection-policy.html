Child Protection Policy

Gia is committed to providing a safe and inclusive platform for all users, including children and young people. This policy outlines the measures taken to protect children from harm, exploitation, and inappropriate content while engaging with Gia’s services.

This policy applies to all Gia staff, content creators, users, and third-party partners. It is designed to meet global standards for child protection and adheres to relevant legal and regulatory requirements.

  • Child: Any individual under the age of 18.
  • Harm: Any form of physical, emotional, or psychological abuse, neglect, exploitation, or exposure to inappropriate content.
  • The welfare of children is paramount.
  • All children have equal rights to protection, regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, disability, or background.
  • Preventative measures are better than reactive responses.
  • Collaboration with legal authorities and child protection organizations will be prioritized when necessary.

Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or to report an issue, please contact Gia’s Child Protection Officer at contact@gia.com.
