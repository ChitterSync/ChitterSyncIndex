How to Post on ChitterSync

To post on ChitterSync, you first need to have an account.

Step 1: Create an Account

- If you donâ€™t already have an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. - Follow the instructions to set up your account.

Step 2: Log In

- Once your account is created, click the "Log In" button in the top right corner and enter your credentials.

Step 3: Create a Post

- After logging in, click the "New Post" button at the top right of the page. - Enter the title and content of your post. - Click the "Post" button to publish it.

Commenting on Posts

- To engage with others, you can comment on their posts. - Click the "Comment" button below the post, type your comment in the text box, and click "Post" to submit it.

Need Help?

- If you have any questions or need assistance, click the "Contact Us" button. You can find it by clicking your profile picture in the navigation bar.
